local_airport Can I check prices in different currencies?

Yes - you can browse Skyscanner in over 150 currencies.

If you're on our main desktop website, you can change the currency by clicking on the country flag in the top menu of the screen and selecting your chosen currency from the drop-down list.

To change currency on our mobile website, tap then tap the country flag and choose your preferred currency.

To do this on the app select then selectand choose your currency from there.

In some cases when linking through to the travel provider’s site, they might display the price in a different currency. This can usually be changed on their site, but if you do end up buying in a different currency, we recommend checking with your bank to see what exchange rate they will apply before making your booking.
